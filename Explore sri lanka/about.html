About Explore Sri Lanka

Your Trusted Travel Companion

Welcome to Explore Sri Lanka

We are passionate about revealing the breathtaking beauty, vibrant culture, and genuine hospitality of Sri Lanka to travelers from across the globe. Our mission is to guide you in discovering both the hidden treasures and the iconic attractions of this tropical paradise, creating unforgettable journeys filled with wonder, warmth, and authentic experiences.

Our Story

Founded by passionate travelers and Sri Lanka enthusiasts, Explore Sri Lanka was created to bridge the gap between international visitors and the authentic experiences this beautiful island has to offer.

With years of experience in the tourism industry and deep-rooted connections throughout the country, we provide insider knowledge and personalized recommendations to make your journey unforgettable.

From the ancient cities of Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa to the pristine beaches of Mirissa and Unawatuna, we help you experience the best of Sri Lanka.
