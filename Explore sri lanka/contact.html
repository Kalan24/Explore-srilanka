Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Sri Lanka?

The best time depends on which coast you're visiting. The west and south coasts are ideal from December to March, while the east coast is best from April to September.

Do I need a visa to visit Sri Lanka?

Most visitors require an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) which can be obtained online before traveling. Check with your local embassy for specific requirements.

How many days do I need to explore Sri Lanka?

We recommend at least 7-10 days to cover the main highlights, though 2-3 weeks allows for a more comprehensive exploration of the island.

Is Sri Lanka safe for solo travelers?

Yes, Sri Lanka is generally safe for solo travelers. The locals are friendly and welcoming. As with any destination, practice common sense and stay aware of your surroundings.
